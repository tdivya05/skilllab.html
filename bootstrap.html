TERRIFIER MOVIE

Terrifier is an American horror media franchise consisting of slasher-splatter films,[1][2][3] comic books, and various other media. The franchise mainly focuses on Sienna Shaw, a young woman destined to defeat the enigmatic Art the Clown, a demonic serial killer that inhabits the fictitious Miles County, New York.

THE NUN

The Nun is a 2018 American gothic supernatural horror film from a story by Dauberman and James Wan.[2][3] It serves as a spiritual spin-off to The Conjuring 2 and is the fifth installment in The Conjuring shared universe. The plot follows a Roman Catholic priest and a nun in her novitiate as they uncover an unholy secret in 1952 Romania..

WINNIE THE POOH:BLOOD & HONEY

Winnie-the-Pooh: Blood and Honey is a 2023 British independent slasher film. It follows Pooh and Piglet, who have become feral murderers, as they terrorise a group of young university women and Christopher Robin when he returns to the Hundred Acre Wood five years after leaving for college.
